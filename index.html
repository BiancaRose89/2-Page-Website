Why are they the best dogs?

The French bulldog can go from smiles and snuggles on the couch to tearing around the house and booty-scooting across the floor in a heartbeat.

Learn More

Description

  • Size : Small, with most adult dogs weighing between 11-13kg.
  • Exercise : Up to one hour a day
  • Life Span : 10-12 years
  • Coat : short coat

Personality

French Bulldogs are quirky and PLAYFUL!

Suitable for families with or without children, they can also get along with cats and other pets if properly introduced.

French Bulldogs rarely bark and, while they need some exercise, they don’t require much, making them popular among city dwellers.

You should be prepared for some hands-on cleaning and grooming rituals, and your schedule should allow for lots of time spent at home to prevent separation anxiety, which French Bulldogs can be prone to.
